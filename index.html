About me

I am a self-taught frontend developer based in Rotterdam, passionate about building interfaces that are as accessible as they are beautiful.

My journey started with curiosity about how websites work under the hood. That curiosity turned into a structured learning path covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React - always with a focus on performance, accessibility and clean code.

When I am not coding, you will find me reading about design systems, contributing to open source projects or experimenting with creative CSS.
